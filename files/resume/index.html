Cover letter

The bootcamp Digital Healthcare with Bridgwater and Taunton College was a great opportunity for me to take a step into the world of technologie. 10 intense weeks with 9 modules focusing on Digital in Healthcare sector have given me knowledge and skills to build my project "Work out - live better".  

As we all know, it is recommended that older people take exercise as well as any prescribed medicines on a regular basis. With the application "Work out - live better", older people can earn Hooray points from every single step they take. These Hooray points can then be donated to a good cause of their choice or used towards shopping with our partner. This application encourages people to take regular exercise and be more mindful of their daily health.   

This application can be attractive to insurance companies for several reasons :  

- Help to build a healthy customer's community.   

- Ensure growth as there are less expenses for customers' health problems.  

- Build up a good national brand image and notoriety.  

- Build up a strong partnership with other brands, such as Morrison’s, Waitrose and charities.  

I am looking forward to presenting my project to you. In the mean time, you are invited to discover the project as well as other modules that I have learnt through my porfolio.

My most recent academic achievements have been to complete a 12 week long Digital Healthcare Bootcamp with Bridgwater and Taunton College.
